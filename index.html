How the Skyshield Funnel Works

Your roofing leads move through a guided funnel — from online ad to booked inspection — without getting lost in messages or notes.

  1. Online Ad / Search — Homeowners click through from Google, Facebook, or direct search.
  2. Landing Page — They see your services, roofing visuals, and trust badges.
  3. Smart Form — They submit address, roof type, and urgency level.
  4. Automated Follow-up — SMS/email confirms the request and offers time slots.
  5. Roof Assessment & Proposal — You inspect, propose, and upsell system upgrades.
